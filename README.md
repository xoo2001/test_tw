# Custom Recovery Tree

Build your own custom recovery (TWRP) for Infinix GT 10 Pro 5G (aka Infinix-X6739 or just X6739).

![Infinix GT Pro 5G](https://fdn2.gsmarena.com/vv/pics/infinix/infinix-gt-10-pro-1.jpg)

|Basic               |Spec Sheet                                                    |
|--                  |--                                                            |
|CPU                 |Octa-core (1x3.0 GHz Cortex-A78 & 3x2.6 GHz Cortex-A78 & 4x2.0 GHz Cortex-A55))      |
|Chipset             |Mediatek Dimensity 8050 (6 nm)                                     |
|GPU                 |Mali-G77 MC9                                              |
|Memory              |8/12GB RAM                                                     |
|Android Version     |13 (XOS 13)                                               |
|Storage             |256GB                                                      |

## Working Feature
- Internal Storage (Decrypted)
- External Storage (SD Card)
- ADB/MTP
